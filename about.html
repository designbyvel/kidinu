Who We Are

A Vertically Integrated Soft Toy Manufacturer, Built for OEM & Private-Label Production.

KID IN U Private Limited is a soft toy manufacturer based in Hosur, Tamil Nadu, operating a dedicated OEM wing for brands and retailers alongside our own KID IN U branded plush toy range.

Every Stage, Under One Roof

Every core stage — cutting, sewing, embroidery, stuffing and finishing — is handled in-house at our Hosur facility, giving our customers tighter control over quality, timelines and confidentiality.

We work as a behind-the-scenes manufacturing partner for OEM customers: you bring the brand and the design, we deliver finished, quality-checked toys ready for your label. Alongside this, we design and produce our own KID IN U branded soft toys.
